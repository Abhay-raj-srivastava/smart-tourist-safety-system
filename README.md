## 📖 Overview

The **Smart Tourist Safety and Incident Response System** is an innovative platform designed to enhance tourist safety through the integration of **Artificial Intelligence (AI), Geofencing Technology, and Blockchain-Based Digital Identity**.

The system aims to provide real-time safety monitoring, intelligent risk assessment, location-aware alerts, secure identity verification, and rapid emergency response services for tourists traveling in unfamiliar environments.

By combining multiple emerging technologies into a unified ecosystem, the platform seeks to reduce response times during emergencies while ensuring secure and trustworthy identity management.

---

## 🎯 Problem Statement

Tourists often face several challenges while traveling, including:

* Personal safety risks in unfamiliar locations
* Delayed emergency response during incidents
* Lack of real-time safety alerts
* Identity verification difficulties
* Limited access to location-specific assistance

Current solutions address these challenges independently. This project proposes an integrated system capable of providing proactive safety monitoring, secure identification, and intelligent incident management.
